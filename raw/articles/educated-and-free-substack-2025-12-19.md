## Comprehensive Summary

### Site Purpose & Positioning
"Educated and Free" is a Substack newsletter authored by "Dissident Teacher" aimed at parents seeking to raise children with strong American civic and cultural values. The content is critical of the public school system, framing it as fundamentally failing students and families. The platform provides resources, book recommendations, and analysis to help parents educate their children outside or in opposition to mainstream institutional curricula.

### Most Popular & Recent Content
The site features a series of posts titled **"A School Day in the Life of Your Child"** that critique specific subjects (AP Environmental Science, Physical Education, Honors English) as vehicles for ideological instruction rather than traditional education.
