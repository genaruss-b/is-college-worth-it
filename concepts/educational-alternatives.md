# Educational Alternatives

**Educational alternatives** refer to non-traditional pathways beyond conventional public schooling and, by extension, the traditional four-year college route. This concept encompasses homeschooling, classical education, trade schools, apprenticeships, and self-directed learning.

## Key Arguments

- Public schools are "actively defrauding your family" according to critics like [Dissident Teacher](entity/dissident-teacher.md)
- Parents are encouraged to take control of their children's education
- The "Western canon" provides a proven cultural and intellectual foundation
- Book-based learning can be more effective than institutional instruction

## Substack as Platform

Substack has become a hub for alternative education voices, with newsletters like Educated and Free providing:

- Curated reading lists (over 400 books)
- Framework for building character and knowledge
- Community support for parents
