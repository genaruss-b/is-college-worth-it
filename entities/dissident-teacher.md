# Dissident Teacher

**Dissident Teacher** is the pseudonymous author of the Substack newsletter [Educated and Free](https://educatedandfree.substack.com/), which supports parents seeking to raise children with strong American civic and cultural values. The author is critical of public schools, arguing they are "actively defrauding your family."

## Key Information

- **Platform:** Substack
- **Subscribers:** Over 8,000
- **Mission:** "Support for families who want their children to be Great Americans"
- **Focus:** Classical/Western canon education alternatives for parents
- **Content Type:** Educational criticism, book recommendations, analysis

## Notable Posts

- "A Library to Build Great Americans, Ages 3-99+" (Dec 13, 2025) — 654 likes, 33 comments, 94 restacks
- "Learning How to Learn" (Jun 15, 2025) — 289 likes
- "On Good Authority" (Oct 13, 2024) — 307 likes
- "The Forever War" (Mar 14, 2025) — discusses Orwell's *1984* and historical narrative control

## Positioning

The teacher frames public education as ideologically compromised and advocates for parental control over children's education. The newsletter provides resources to help parents educate their children outside mainstream institutional curricula.
